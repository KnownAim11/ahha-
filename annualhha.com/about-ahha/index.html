About AHHA

Honoring Heroes Who Make a Difference

01
29.7604° N, 95.3698° W
HOUSTON, TX

I nspired by the heartfelt stories of courageous individuals, police officers, and first responders who risk their lives daily, of the tireless public servants committed to making a difference, those whose mission and passion are to serve in their communities by helping others, RAK day had to be the platform for the unsung heroes of our city and Nation!

In celebration of Houston's Random Acts of Kindness day and with generous support from Royal Sonesta Houston Hotel, the Annual Houston Humanitarian Awards was established to recognize local and National heroes that exemplify kindness by giving back to their communities. Recognized by the city of Houston and Mayor Sylvester Turner, the humanitarian awards elegant black-tie gala honors ten individuals who have given of themselves unselfishly. The ten final honoree selections are chosen by an advisory board of past recipients, selected city council members, and participating sponsors. To submit nominees for award considerations, please fill out this form.
